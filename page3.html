SAVE THE STRAYS

In this gallery you will see collections of photographs of stray cats and dogs that we have seen around the streets of Bulacan. At every end of the slide is the description of the shown animals below. 
Every photo taken is captured and edited by Vivien De Gracia. 

Cats of BulSU

Cats in BulSU is a normal thing to see everyday. They strive on the excess and thrown food by the stores inside the campus. They take shelter within the walls of the campus.
